Teaching & Program Development

My pedagogical approach is centered on problem-based learning, blending theoretical frameworks with hands-on application. In addition to the courses I am teaching, I am an active member of the program development team for the new "AI in Business" Master's Program at Copenhagen Business School. Besides co-creating the overall program, I am responsible for the courses "Strategy and AI" as well as the "AI Venture Lab". I have designed and taught a wide variety of courses at the MBA, Master, and Bachelor levels at Copenhagen Business School (CBS) and Vienna University of Economics and Business (WU Vienna).
